About

I am a strategic business leader with a proven track record in driving transformation, operational excellence, and sustainable growth across Fintech, IT, Mobility Tech, Banking, and Investment sectors throughout EMEA and Pan-Africa. My expertise spans P&L and budget management, operations leadership, board and government relations, credit and risk management, investment strategy, and business intelligence—delivering measurable outcomes at every stage. I have successfully spearheaded high-impact operations, aligning business goals with board-level strategy while managing multi-million dollar budgets and credit portfolios. Notably, I oversaw a $70 million credit portfolio across various financing models—including BNPL, hire purchase, and rent-to-own—implementing structured risk mitigation and reducing non-performing loans. In the investment landscape, I’ve supported early to Series C fundraising for Pan-African fintech and mobility ventures, optimizing capital deployment and portfolio expansion. My stakeholder management experience includes leading external relations with government bodies, regulators, and corporate boards, ensuring compliance and fostering strategic partnerships. By applying data-driven insights and analytics engineering, I’ve enhanced operational decisions, accelerated revenue growth, and improved performance across multiple markets. I’ve also led infrastructure and supply chain optimization initiatives, driving efficiency, reducing turnaround times, and maximizing resource utilization. With a customer-centric approach, I’ve designed strategies that improved user experience, boosted retention, and generated over $10M in revenue growth. I’ve managed and empowered cross-functional teams across 16 cities in four countries, cultivating operational excellence and team performance. Academically, I am a PhD candidate with an MBA and a Bachelor's degree, continually expanding my expertise in business transformation, analytics, and strategic execution. I’m passionate about building scalable business models, optimizing operations, and leveraging data to unlock long-term growth. Let’s connect to explore how we can create impact together.
